Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy is a plain-language starting point, not a substitute for legal advice. Have a lawyer familiar with Nigerian data protection law (the NDPA and NDPC regulations) review and adapt it before you rely on it.

1. What We Collect

Depending on which form you use on this site, we may collect:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Program interest, application details, and any message you write to us
  • For Licensing applications specifically: a copy of your training certificate, a government ID (NIN slip or international passport), and a live selfie photo
  • Payment-related information processed through Paystack (we do not see or store your full card details — that's handled entirely by Paystack)
  • Basic technical information like your browser type and general location, for security and to keep the site working properly

2. Why We Collect It

  • To process your application, admission, and enrollment
  • To verify your identity and qualifications for the Licensing program specifically
  • To process payments and confirm your seat in a program
  • To create and secure your student portal account, once payment is confirmed
  • To respond to questions sent through the Contact page or FAQ
  • To meet our own record-keeping and regulatory obligations as a training institution

3. Where It's Stored

Your data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore and Cloud Storage), Google's cloud infrastructure. Login-code emails are sent through Gmail. Payments are processed by Paystack, a licensed Nigerian payment processor — we don't store your card or bank login details ourselves.

4. Who Can See It

Your application and documents are visible only to authorized CGTIA staff reviewing admissions and licensing, plus the third-party processors above strictly for the purpose of doing their job (Paystack for payment, Google for hosting/email delivery). We do not sell your information, and we do not share it with advertisers.

5. How Long We Keep It

We keep application and enrollment records for as long as needed for academic, regulatory, and accreditation purposes. If your application isn't approved or you don't proceed with payment, we'll retain your submission for a reasonable period for our own records before it's eligible for deletion on request.

6. Your Rights

You can ask us to:

  • Show you what data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data, where we're not required to keep it for legal or academic record-keeping reasons

Reach out through the Contact page to make any of these requests.

7. Cookies

This site does not use advertising or tracking cookies. Any cookies set are strictly functional (for example, keeping you signed in to the student portal).

8. Children's Privacy

Our programs are intended for applicants 18 years of age and older. We don't knowingly collect information from anyone younger.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the site and our programs evolve. We'll update the date at the top of this page when we do.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent through the Contact page or to admissions@cgtia.org.
