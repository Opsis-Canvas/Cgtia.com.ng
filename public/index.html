Caregivers Training & Internship Academy Limited (CGTIA) equips everyday people with certified caregiving skills, hands-on clinical practice, and guided internship placements — building careers rooted in dignity and care.

Our Approach to Caregiving Education

Caregiving is a skill that deserves proper training, not just good intentions. CGTIA blends structured classroom learning with hands-on clinical practice and supervised internships, so every graduate leaves ready to provide safe, competent, and compassionate care — whether in a client's home, a care facility, or a hospital setting.
